About

Project

SEM is an ongoing project to restore and improve an electron microsope purchased off ebay. Currently the microscope is easily usable, although magnification levels are not as high as desired. Work is being done to improve the digitization of the analog images produced. So far the Scanning Electron Microscope has been used primarily for still life images, although one student has produced a video of bactererial osmosis.
